Salmon tart

Description

A salmon and spinach tart with a pre-made puff-pastry

Ingredients

  • 1/2 of a standard roll of pre-made puff-pastry
  • 400ml of 12-18% fat sour cream
  • 1 piece of salmon (fresh of smoked)
  • 2-3 eggs
  • 1 cup of fresh spinach leaves
  • grated cheese
  • salt, pepper, nutmeg

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven
  2. Paste the puff-pastry into the round tart form, to create the bottom and side layer of the tart.
  3. Pre-bake it for 10 min at 240 Celsius degrees
  4. If the salmon is fresh: bake / fry it
  5. Blanch the spinach
  6. In na bowl mix sour cream with grated cheese and spinach. Mix together with eggs. Add spices
  7. Put the salmon into the pre-baked puff-pastry
  8. Add the content of the bowl over the salmon
  9. Bake for ~15 min at 200 Celsius degrees
  10. Serve while hot, or in a room temperature
