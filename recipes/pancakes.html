Pancakes

Description

Simple, fluffy pancakes, to be served with any toppings. Works well with a maple sirup, honey and a whole variety of fruits

Serves 2-3 people

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of plain wheat flour (without the baking powder)
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • pinch of salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 tablespoons of oil
  • some oil / other fat for frying

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl mix the flour with the baking powder and salt.
  2. In a bigger bowl beat eggs with sugar.
  3. Into the bigger bowl add oil and milk and stir (whisk/blend/mix) until the foam appears.
  4. Add dry ingredients to the wet ones and mix quickly (for not too long, because it makes pancakes hard).
  5. Heat the frying pan with a small amount of fat. Place a few ~10-15cm diameter blobs of dough into it using a ladle.
  6. Fry until small bubbles will start appearing on the surface, and the edges become slightly dry. Then flip it to the other side. A nicely done ones are slightly brown on both sides.
  7. Serve when hot, with toppings of your choice.
