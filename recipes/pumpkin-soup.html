Pumpkin soup

Description

Easy and tasty Hokkaido pumpkin and vegetables soup

Ingredients

  • 1/2 of a medium Hokkaido pumpkin
  • 3 small carrots
  • 1 small onion
  • ~1,5cm of a ginger root
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1 broth cube / fresh bulion
  • 1 standard can of a coconut milk
  • 1/2 lemon
  • salt, pepper, soy sauce

Steps

  1. Cut the pumpkin, carrots and onion. Grate the ginger.
  2. In a medium/large pot stew (or fry) together pumpkin, carrots, onion, ginger and butter.
  3. Pour bulion/ bulion made with a broth cube over the vegetables, so the liquid covers them completely.
  4. Boil until the vegetables are soft
  5. Add cocoa milk, lemon juice and other spices.
  6. Blend it
  7. Serve hot
